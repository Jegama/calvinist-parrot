#### **Section 3: The "Nurture & Admonition" Log**

*From Ephesians 6:4: "Bring them up in the nurture and admonition of the Lord." Both terms describe parental actions, not the child's state.*

* **Log Entry Types:**
  * **Nurture:** Record times you caught them doing good and how you blessed them.
    * *Example:* "Edmund reached for the outlet, I said No, and he pulled back! We clapped and read a book."
  * **Admonition:** Record times correction was needed and how you shepherded their heart.
    * *Example:* "Threw food on the floor. Mealtime ended immediately. Explained that food is a gift from God."

* **Why this naming:**
  * "Nurture" = positive reinforcement of godly character (celebrating obedience)
  * "Admonition" (Greek: *nouthesia*) = "putting in mind", instruction and warning that redirects to the right path
  * Both frame what *parents do*, not what the child is "sowing"

* **Fields per Log:**
  * Date (auto-filled)
  * Category (Nurture / Admonition)
  * What happened (short narrative)
  * Gospel Connection: "How did we point him to Jesus?"
  * Author (which parent logged it)
  * Tags (optional: circumstance, heart issue, virtue)

#### **Section 5: The Monthly Review (Dashboard)**

*A visual summary for the parents to discuss on date night. Versioned by year-month.*

* **Trend Line:** Are we seeing growth in [Character Goal]?
* **Consistency Check:** Have we (parents) been consistent with the Blessings/Consequences plan?
* **Top Heart Issues:** Recurring themes from logs
* **Action Step:** What do we need to adjust for next month?

---

#### **Section 6: The Annual Review (Date Night Mode)**

*Prompted annually (e.g., January 1st or child's birthday).*

* **Prompt:** "It is [Date]. Time to review the plan."
* **Assessment:** Did we see growth in [Character Goal]? [Competency Goal]?
* **Bracket Check:** Is the child approaching a new age bracket? Do we need to adjust our approach?
  * *Example:* "Edmund is moving from the 0–3 bracket toward 3–6 soon. Do we need to update our Competencies to include 'Cleaning up toys'?"
* **Plan Forward:** Create next year's Four Elements plan (old year becomes read-only history)
* **Prayer Prompt:** Option to generate a prayer focus for the child for the coming year
