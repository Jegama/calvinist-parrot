# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 6, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

Calvinist Parrot ("we," "us," or "our") is a ministry project of [Calvinist Parrot Ministries](https://www.calvinistparrotministries.org/). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

- **Name** (display name)
- **Email address**
- **Password** (stored securely by our authentication provider, never accessible to us in plain text)

If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from your Google account. We do not receive or store your Google password.

### Content You Create

When you use our features, we store the content you provide:

- **Chat conversations:** Your questions and the AI-generated responses
- **Journal entries:** Personal and discipleship journal text, along with AI-generated pastoral reflections and prayer suggestions
- **Prayer requests:** Personal and family prayer request text, prayer history, and rotation schedules
- **Kids discipleship:** Annual plans, monthly visions, and discipleship observations for children in your household
- **Family member information:** Names, relationships, and birth dates of household members you add to the prayer tracker

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Cookies:** We use essential cookies for authentication (session token), anonymous guest identity, and OAuth flow state. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. See Section 5 for details.
- **Performance data:** We use Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights to collect anonymized page views, Core Web Vitals, and general performance metrics. This data is not tied to your identity.
- **User agent:** Your browser's user-agent string is forwarded to our authentication provider for session security purposes.

### AI Memory

Our chat system learns your theological interests, preferred topics, and general spiritual context across conversations to provide more personalized pastoral responses. This memory is:

- Tied to your user account and never shared with other users
- Extracted automatically from your conversations using AI
- Stored in our database alongside your profile

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Calvinist Parrot service:

- **Deliver our features:** Power chat conversations, journal reflections, prayer tracking, kids discipleship planning, and church discovery
- **Personalize responses:** Tailor AI responses based on your denomination preference, theological interests, and conversation history
- **Generate AI content:** Process your questions, journal entries, and discipleship logs through AI models to provide pastoral reflections, prayer suggestions, and theological guidance
- **Maintain your account:** Authenticate your sessions, manage password resets, and sync data across devices
- **Improve the service:** Analyze anonymized performance metrics to improve speed and reliability

We **never** use your data for advertising, sell your data to third parties, or train AI models with your personal content.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Calvinist Parrot. Your data is shared with them only as necessary to provide the service:

| Service | Data Shared | Purpose |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Appwrite** (Authentication) | Name, email, session tokens | User authentication and account management |
| **OpenAI** | Chat messages, journal entries, conversation context | AI-powered theological responses, pastoral reflections, and memory extraction |
| **Google Gemini** | Chat messages, church website content | AI-powered theological responses and church doctrinal evaluations |
| **Tavily** | Search queries, church website URLs | Web search for supplemental theological articles, church website analysis, and daily devotional generation |
| **Vercel** | Anonymized performance metrics | Hosting, analytics, and speed monitoring |
| **Neon** (PostgreSQL) | All stored data | Database hosting |
| **Geoapify** | Church addresses | Geocoding church locations for the church finder map |
| **AO Lab Bible API** | Bible verse references | Retrieving Scripture text and commentaries (no personal data sent) |

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data. When your content is sent to AI providers (OpenAI, Google Gemini), it is processed according to their API terms of service, which generally prohibit using API inputs for model training.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained as long as your account is active.
- **Chat history, journal entries, prayer requests, and discipleship data:** Retained as long as your account exists. You can delete individual conversations from the chat sidebar.
- **AI memory:** Retained alongside your profile and updated as you have new conversations.
- **Guest data:** Anonymous chat sessions are identified by a guest cookie that expires after one year.
- **Performance analytics:** Retained according to Vercel's data retention policies.

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at [contact@calvinistparrotministries.org](mailto:contact@calvinistparrotministries.org).

---

## 5. Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies, all essential for the service to function:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|--------|---------|----------|------|
| Session cookie | Authenticates your logged-in session | Session-based | Essential |
| Guest ID | Identifies anonymous users for chat continuity | 1 year | Essential |
| Auth intent | Tracks OAuth login/signup flow state | 10 minutes | Essential |

We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking across websites, or any non-essential purpose.

---

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

- Passwords are hashed and managed by Appwrite; we never have access to plain-text passwords
- Session cookies are HTTP-only and secure (in production), preventing client-side script access
- API routes require server-side authentication before accessing any user data
- Database connections use SSL encryption
- Rate limiting is applied to sensitive endpoints such as password recovery

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Calvinist Parrot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Kids Discipleship feature is designed for **parents** to track their children's spiritual growth; parents enter information about their children as part of their own account.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information directly, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information in your profile
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [contact@calvinistparrotministries.org](mailto:contact@calvinistparrotministries.org).
